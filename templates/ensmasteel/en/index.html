Who are we ?

Passionate about robotics and engineering, we're a group of students determined to take on tomorrow's technological challenges. Every year, we enthusiastically prepare to take part in the Coupe de France de Robotique, the leading event in the field in France.

Through our projects to design, build and program competitive robots, we put into practice the knowledge acquired during our engineering training. The Coupe de France de Robotique is an opportunity for us to demonstrate our technical skills, team spirit and creativity.

We are proud to represent ISAE-ENSMA in this competition. Our aim is to push back the boundaries of robotics by developing innovative, high-performance solutions.

Development
Here are the main development areas carried out each year for the Coupe de France de Robotique project
CAO
Catia V5 is our software of choice for the mechanical design of our robot. The know-how that the school's training provides enables us to design our robots with ease.
Electronics
Each year we design and assemble our own printed circuit boards to meet the needs of our robots.
Programming
Mainly C++, but going towards a ROS2 architecture.
